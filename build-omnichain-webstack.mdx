### **Core Benefits of the Omnichain Web**

- **For Developers:** A scalable, secure, and user-friendly environment to build omnichain applications.
- **For Protocols:** Unified liquidity and cross-chain interoperability for enhanced scalability and adoption.
- **For Users:** Seamless, efficient interactions across blockchains with reduced complexity.
