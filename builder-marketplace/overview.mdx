In the Builder Marketplace of Omnichain Web, Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) play a critical role in ensuring secure and reliable execution of intent protocols and AI agents. TEEs provide a hardware-based, isolated environment that prevents unauthorized access, preserving the integrity and confidentiality of sensitive data during computation. By utilizing TEEs, Omnichain Web safeguards both input data and the execution process from potential threats, enhancing trust in cross-chain operations.
